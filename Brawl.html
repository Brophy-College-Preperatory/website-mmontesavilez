Brawl Stars

Background

Supercell, on December 12, 2018, came out with one of their games called Brawl Stars. Brawl Stars is a mobile multiplayer online battle arena video. It originally didn't have many active users, but after 6 years it has grown to 376 million players. Brawl stars uses a battle pass and season system that has made it grown a lot. The developers also like to listen to their community to a lot and this has also played a part in their game growing to over 300 milion players.
